# 📈 CCSS Math K.CC — 2026 Product Launch Plan

A strategic, quarterly roadmap to launch and scale high-converting TPT products aligned with Kindergarten Counting & Cardinality standards.

---

## ✅ Core Principles
- **No upload gaps >2 days** — maintain algorithm visibility
- **Bundle every 6–8 products** — boost AOV & cross-sell
- **1 product ≤ 3 hrs** (Phase 1) → target ≤ 1.5 hrs by Q3
- **All assets follow `pack-spec.md` & `release-checklist.md`**

---

## 📅 Q1: Foundation & Traction (Jan–Mar)
**Goal:** 24 products + 3 bundles

| Week | Focus | Output |
|--------|--------|---------|
| 1–2 | Quick Wins (Phase 1) | `K.CC.B.5`, `K.CC.C.7`, `K.CC.A.3`, `K.CC.B.4` × 2 themes = **8 products** |
| 3–4 | Theme Expansion (Phase 2) | 8 base products × 3 themes (Animals/Space/Ocean) = **24 products** |
| End Q1 | Packaging & SEO | `Counting Skills Bundle`, `Numbers 0–20 Bundle`, `K.CC Starter Bundle`, SEO audit |

---

## 📅 Q2: Scale & Authority (Apr–Jun)
**Goal:** +30 products + 2 curriculum packs

- Complete all remaining K.CC standards (`A.1`, `A.2`, `C.6`, `B.4c`, etc.)
- Add *Differentiation*: Challenge/Intervention versions per standard
- Launch `K.CC Curriculum Pack`: includes assessment, tracking sheet, teacher guide, answer keys

---

## 📅 Q3: Monetization & Automation (Jul–Sep)
**Goal:** Reduce production time, add seasonal demand

- Build `create-pack.sh`: auto-generate folder, replace placeholders, validate structure
- Launch `product-tracker.md`: live status table (Standard, Name, Status, TPT URL, Date)
- Release seasonal bundles: `Back-to-School Counting`, `Halloween Numbers 0–20`, `Thanksgiving Comparing`

---

## 📅 Q4: Growth & Ecosystem (Oct–Dec)
**Goal:** Compound revenue & brand authority

- Launch `CCSS K Math Starter Kit`: bundle of 4 domains (Counting, Operations, Measurement, Geometry)
- Publish free lead magnets: printable cheat sheets, parent guides, mini-courses → collect emails → convert to buyers
- Integrate AI skill to generate personalized listing previews & thumbnails on upload
