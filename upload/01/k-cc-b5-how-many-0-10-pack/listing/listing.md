CCSS K.CC.B.5 How Many? Counting Worksheets (0-10)

This printable pack targets **CCSS Math Kindergarten Counting & Cardinality: K.CC.B.5**.

- Answering “How many?” by writing the total (0-10)
- Identifying the correct total (multiple choice)

Includes:

- Credits page (for final export)

Perfect for:

- Math centers
- Morning work
- Small groups
- Intervention
